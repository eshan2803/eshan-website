Driving the Future of Clean Energy

From deep science to real-world impact

I'm Eshan, a scientist and engineer dedicated to accelerating the transition to a sustainable energy future. My work focuses on developing and scaling innovative technologies in renewable energy systems, from next-generation fuels to carbon capture. With a foundation in rigorous academic research and a passion for tangible impact, I bridge the gap between laboratory discoveries and commercial viability.

My mission is to leverage deep scientific expertise to solve critical challenges in energy storage and conversion. I aim to create scalable, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions that combat climate change and power our world cleanly and efficiently.
