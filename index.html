About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Eshan Singh — an energy and decarbonization expert with over a decade of experience working at the intersection of technology, industry, and research. I have led cross-disciplinary projects in carbon capture, hydrogen integration, renewable energy systems, and sustainable transportation across organizations like Pacific Gas & Electric, Meta Platforms, and Sandia National Laboratories.

With a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering and a portfolio spanning peer-reviewed publications, patents, and invited talks worldwide, I am passionate about driving innovative, scalable pathways toward a carbon-neutral future. Through this platform, I share insights on clean energy technologies, sustainability strategies, and the future of climate innovation.

Mission Statement

My mission is to bridge scientific innovation with real-world application to advance the global transition to clean energy. I am committed to developing scalable, impactful solutions in carbon capture, hydrogen energy, and sustainable systems that accelerate decarbonization while ensuring economic and environmental resilience for future generations.
