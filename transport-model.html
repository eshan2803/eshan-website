Commodity Transport Model

This tool allows you to calculate the energy usage, carbon footprint, and cost of transporting commodities across different global trade routes. It integrates fuel types, transport modes (e.g., ship, rail, truck), and route distances.

Features include:

  • Distance-aware energy and emissions estimation
  • Support for maritime, rail, and road transport
  • Cost optimization and comparative mode analysis
